🆘 How to Use SMART Goal Tracker

🎯 What is a SMART Goal?

A SMART goal is Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound. This app helps you break down your goals and track progress effectively.

📝 Adding a Goal

  • Go to the "Add Goal" page.
  • Fill in all SMART components.
  • Click Add Goal to save it.

📋 Viewing & Managing Goals

  • Click "View Goals" in the navbar to see your saved goals.
  • Use the Edit, Delete, or Complete/Undo buttons to manage each goal.
  • Use the ➕ button under each goal to log check-ins and monitor progress.
